FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Do I need accounting knowledge to use Zande?
+
Not at all. Zande is designed for people who run businesses, not people who studied accounting. Everything uses plain language — if you can fill out a basic form, you can use Zande. We explain what each thing means as you go.
Is it really free during the beta?
+
Yes. Create an account and use the full platform — no payment required at any stage of testing. We haven't set pricing yet. We want to speak to real users first and price it fairly based on what people actually find valuable.
Is Zande built for South Africa specifically?
+
Entirely. ZAR currency, SA bank formats, SARS VAT201 calculations, two-monthly VAT periods, and data hosted in Cape Town under POPIA. Not a foreign product with SA tax bolted on as an afterthought.
Is my financial data safe?
+
Yes. All data is hosted in Supabase's Cape Town region (af-south-1), encrypted at rest and in transit. Row-level security means your data is fully isolated from other users. POPIA compliant. We never sell your data, ever.
Do I need to be VAT registered to use Zande?
+
No. Zande works for both VAT-registered and non-VAT businesses. If you're not registered, invoices simply skip the VAT lines. When you register with SARS, change one setting and everything updates automatically.
